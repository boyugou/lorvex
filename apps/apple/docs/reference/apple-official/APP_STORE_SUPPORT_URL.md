# App Store Support URL

Source: [Platform version information](https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/reference/app-information/platform-version-information/)

Last verified: 2026-07-10

## Apple Contract

The Support URL is required for an App Store version. Apple says it must lead
to actual contact information so users can reach the developer about issues,
feedback, and feature requests. App Review Guideline 1.5 separately requires
the app and Support URL to provide an easy way to contact the developer.

## Lorvex Mapping

Lorvex now uses the stable, public, login-free
`https://lorvex.app/support/` page as its Support URL. The page is independent
of GitHub repository visibility and gives users an actionable route for issues,
feedback, and feature requests. The public product policy still has no email
mailbox.

## Release Boundary

The Support URL decision is resolved. Two separate account-level requirements
remain: App Review Information needs a real private reviewer phone/email, and an
EU trader declaration can require Apple to publish legal contact details. The
public support page does not replace either field; neither requires inventing a
public email address in the app or repository.
